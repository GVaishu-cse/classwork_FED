LMS

FEDF-PS10: Develop a unique and effective learning management system (LMS)

Create a comprehensive LMS for managing online education — for educators, content creators, and students.

Goal

Build an app to handle user roles, course content creation and publishing, and tools that make it easy for educators to deliver and manage online learning effectively.

Core features
  • Create & manage courses and lessons
  • Role-based access & user management
  • Assignments, grading & progress tracking
  • Content creation & publishing workflow

User roles

Admin

Manage platform settings, user roles, permissions, and oversee course content and platform-level analytics.

Instructor

Create and manage courses, upload lessons and resources, create and grade assignments, and interact with students via messages & forums.

Student

Enroll in courses, complete lessons, submit assignments, view grades, and track learning progress and achievements.

Content Creator

Develop course materials, update multimedia content, collaborate with instructors, and ensure educational quality and accessibility.
